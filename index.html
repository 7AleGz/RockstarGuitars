Rockstar Guitars

In the mid-1980s, when interest in electric guitars used in rock increased, Rockstar Guitars got open a gap with respect to many of its competitors, with new more specialized guitars, offering new technical improvements, unlike its competitors. The company struck deals with many rock stars of the time, and this led to another big rise on sales. Rockstar guitars are designed with the demands of the discernible player in mind, Rockstar Guitars give you the shapes, finishes and tones you love. From beginners to the world's most influential artists, our instruments are the #1 choice for guitarists around the world. From beginner guitars, affordable high-quality import guitars, as well as the best US-built guitars, Rockstar Guitars makes a guitar that is for you.
